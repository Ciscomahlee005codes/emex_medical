At Emex Medical Supply Limited, our success is driven by a dedicated team of experienced professionals who bring their expertise, leadership, and passion for excellence to every aspect of our business. Meet the individuals who guide our company with vision and integrity.


Mr. Okolo Emmanuel Chukwuemeka


Founder, Emex Medical Supply Limited


Mr. Okolo Emmanuel Chukwuemeka Founder of Emex Medical Supply Limited, is a visionary entrepreneur with a deep passion for health innovations and the stock market. With a commitment to revolutionizing the medical supply chain, he founded the company to bridge gaps in healthcare by providing seamless, tech-enabled access to high-quality medical supplies. Under his leadership, Emex Medical Supply Limited has grown into a trusted name in the industry, ensuring that healthcare providers receive reliable, affordable, and timely solutions to meet their critical needs.

Co-Founder Emex Supply Limited

His leadership in operations ensures that the company's logistics run smoothly, delivering reliable medical products to clients worldwide. His deep expertise in operations management, creative strategy, and healthcare advocacy has been instrumental in driving the company's mission of providing high-quality medical supplies and fostering positive change in healthcare solutions.
